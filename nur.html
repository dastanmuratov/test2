Welcome to Hilton!

Hilton Astana is a 4-star hotel in Astana with an indoor heated pool, indoor swimming pool and restaurant. Lovers Park and Transport Tower are located at a distance of 4.2 km and 4.2 km, respectively. The city centre is 10 km from the hotel. MEGA Silk Way is located near the hotel. Guests can relax in beautiful rooms overlooking the city, equipped with free Wi-Fi and panoramic windows. In the rooms you will find guest bathrooms.
